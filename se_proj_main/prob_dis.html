Probable Disease(s)



1.Common Cold

  • Running nose

  • Apetite loss

  • Headaches

  • Generalized aches and pains

  • Fever

  • Weakness

  • Coughing

  • Diarrhea or constipation.
    •


    2.Typhoid

  • Poor appetite

  • Abdominal pain;

  • Headaches

  • Generalized aches and pains

  • Fever, often up to 104 F

  • Lethargy (usually only if untreated)

  • Intestinal bleeding or perforation
